        <div id="WNStoryBody" class=" withRelated right">
         <p>Florida drivers who have ordered a driver's license or identification card online or by mail no longer need to call the Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles' Customer Service Center to inquire about its whereabouts.</p> 
         <p>The DMV now offers an online tracking system that will allow residents to look up when their license or ID card was printed and mailed.</p> 
         <p>In a statement released by the DMV, Division of Motorist Services Director, Sandra Lambert said, "This has been years in the making, and it is another example of ways we are improving customer service efforts in Florida. We know our customers' time is valuable. We want to help them save time and make doing business with the department easy."</p> 
         <p>To access the system, visit <a href="http://www.flhsmv.gov/">www.flhsmv.gov</a> and select Driver License and ID Card Tracking System. Customers will need their license or ID number to look up their order.</p>
