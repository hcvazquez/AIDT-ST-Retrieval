       <h2>Application</h2> 
       <p class="
                ait4">This unit describes a participant’s skills and knowledge required to conduct civil construction scraper operations in Civil construction.</p> 
       <p class="
                ait4">This unit is appropriate for those working in operational roles.</p> 
       <p class="
                ait4">Licensing, legislative, regulatory and certification requirements that apply to this unit can vary between states, territories, and Industry sectors. Relevant information must be sourced prior to application of the unit.</p> 
       <p class="
                ait4"></p> 
       <h2>Unit Sector</h2> 
       <p class="
                ait4">Civil construction</p>

       <h2>Performance Evidence</h2> 
       <p class="
                ait4">Evidence is required to be collected that demonstrates a candidate’s competency in this unit. Evidence must be relevant to the roles within this sector’s work operations and satisfy all of the requirements of the performance criteria of this unit and include evidence that the candidate:</p>
       <ul class="ait13">
        <li>locates and applies relevant documentation, policies and procedures</li> 
        <li>implements the requirements, procedures and techniques for the safe, effective and efficient completion of scraper operations including:</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait14">
        <li>conducting prestart checks prior to commencing operations and shutdown procedures on completion of operations</li> 
        <li>carrying out vehicle refuelling requirements and procedures</li> 
        <li>driving and operating the equipment to site conditions</li> 
        <li>applying safe work practices and identifying and reporting all potential hazards, risks and environmental issues</li> 
        <li>applying problem solving and troubleshooting techniques</li> 
        <li>applying levelling techniques</li> 
        <li>selecting and using the required tools and equipment</li> 
        <li>working safely around other machines and personnel</li> 
        <li>safely parking and securing of equipment</li> 
        <li>applying methods of changing machine attachments</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait13">
        <li>works effectively with others to undertake and complete scraper operations that meet all of the required outcomes including:</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait14">
        <li>using a range of communications techniques and equipment to convey information to others</li> 
        <li>maintaining written and verbal reporting requirements and procedures</li> 
        <li>organising work activities to meet all task requirements</li> 
        <li>communicating clearly and concisely with others to receive and clarify work instructions</li> 
        <li>coordinating equipment coupling, operating in tandem and disengagement of equipment with other operator</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait13">
        <li>demonstrates completion of civil construction scraper operations that safely, effectively and efficiently meets all of the required outcomes on more than one (1) occasion including:</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait14">
        <li>completion of operations to specification using a variety of material types including:</li>
       </ul>
       <ul class="ait31016">
        <li>stripping of materials to specified depth</li> 
        <li>the cutting and filling of materials to line and level</li> 
        <li>stockpiling</li> 
        <li>spreading</li> 
        <li>site clean-up</li> 
        <li>coupling, operating in tandem and disengagement of equipment in coordination with other operator</li>
       </ul>
       <h2>Knowledge Evidence</h2> 
       <p class="
                ait4">The candidate must demonstrate knowledge of the following when conducting civil construction scraper operations:</p>
       <ul class="ait14">
        <li>identifying, interpreting ground conditions</li> 
        <li>communicating and performing isolation procedures</li> 
        <li>identifying equipment processes, technical capability and limitations</li> 
        <li>being prepared for fire/accident/emergency</li> 
        <li>identifying signs of operator fatigue and how it should be managed</li> 
        <li>interpreting drawings and sketches</li> 
        <li>identifying site isolation and traffic control responsibilities and authorities</li> 
        <li>using civil construction terminology</li> 
        <li>complying with project quality requirements</li> 
        <li>using basic earthworks calculations</li> 
        <li>using civil construction activity sequences of road construction, earthworks and drainage</li> 
        <li>using methods for calculating safe working loads</li> 
        <li>applying operational, maintenance and basic diagnostics</li> 
        <li>completing housekeeping activities</li>
       </ul>
