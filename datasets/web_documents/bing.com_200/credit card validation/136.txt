                <p>Experian's real-time authentication service for merchants is the only industry tool that verifies the full credit card number against a consumer's identity through reported trade line activity.&nbsp; Authentication at the point of purchase is critical to safeguarding your customer's information and your business creating an environment that reduces fraud and enables business growth.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p> 
                <ul> 
                 <li>Automated authentication capabilities easily uncovers fraudulent behavior and creates operational efficiencies.</li> 
                 <li>Unique technology verifies full credit card number against a consumer's identity via reported trade line activity.</li> 
                 <li>Verifies transactions in seconds rather than minutes.</li> 
                 <li>Access to Experian's File One<sup>SM</sup> credit database containing information on more than 220 million credit-active consumers.</li> 
                 <li>Fraud alerts on closed accounts, lost or stolen credit cards, and victim statements.</li> 
                 <li>Multiple access methods include – real-time or batch processing, instant Web-based processing, XML delivery – which allow transparent integration with your internal platforms or consumer-facing Web sites.</li>
