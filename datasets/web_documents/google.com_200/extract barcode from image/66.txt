               <h3><b>DataMan&nbsp;Image-based&nbsp;Barcode Reading Technology</b></h3>
               <div class="tab_spotlight right">
                <div class="postit">
                 <h3>Next Steps</h3>
                 <ul class="cta_links">
                  <li><a href="/contact-sales.aspx?locale=&amp;cm_campid=FD596EDB-CEE5-E311-A12C-5EF3FCDAF1B7">Contact Sales</a></li>

               </div>
               <p>Software is standardized across DataMan products and is designed for ease of use and maximum flexibility. DataMan software includes several features for the DataMan fixed-mount readers that by far surpass other image-based fixed-mount readers on the market.</p>
               <ul>
                <li class="bullet-angle-bracket"><a title="Get Product Pricing" href="/contact-pricing.aspx?locale=&amp;cm_campid=8375BACD-CFE5-E311-A12C-5EF3FCDAF1B7">Get Product Pricing</a></li>
               </ul>
               <p><b>Featured capabilities include:</b></p>
               <ul>
                <li>A high-speed advanced platform that runs the latest proprietary Cognex algorithms for decoding both 1-D and 2-D symbologies</li>
                <li>1DMax with Hotbars II&nbsp; provides high read rates on 1-D barcodes, even those that may be damaged or distorted</li>
                <li>Hotbars II delivers "barcode stitching" technology and 0.8 ppm (pixels per module) decoding speed to help you solve applications with fewer readers and less complexity</li>
                <li>2DMax with patent-pending PowerGrid technology decodes severely damaged or poorly marked 2-D matrix codes, and can even read 2-D codes without visible perimeters or quiet zones</li>
                <li>2DMax&nbsp;with PowerGrid overcomes variations in lighting, marking method, code quality, or surface finish  including damaged finder and clocking patterns and quiet zone violations</li>
                <li>ImageMax technology is specifically designed to read challenging DPM codes on a variety of round, reflective or specular surfaces using a combination of cross polarized and co-linear polarized LEDs and a high powered lens with liquid lens technology.</li>
                <li>Intuitive Setup Tool for the simplest setup and deployment in real time</li>
                <li>Intelligent tuning technology automatically adjusts the settings of the integrated lighting to find the optimal light setup for the part</li>
                <li>Auto-tune and trigger buttons make the readers easy to set up without a PC or HMI</li>
                <li>1-D and 2-D quality metrics for process control monitoring and thresholding</li>
                <li>Various trigger modes (self, single, burst, etc.) to meet the broadest range of customer applications</li>
                <li>JavaScript programming for easiest PLC connection and integration on the factory floor</li>
               </ul>
               <p><b>Proprietary Cognex software algorithms give the DataMan barcode readers unmatched code reading performance:</b></p>
