        <h3> 
         <svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-book" height="16" version="1.1" viewbox="0 0 16 16" width="16">
          <path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M3 5h4v1H3V5zm0 3h4V7H3v1zm0 2h4V9H3v1zm11-5h-4v1h4V5zm0 2h-4v1h4V7zm0 2h-4v1h4V9zm2-6v9c0 .55-.45 1-1 1H9.5l-1 1-1-1H2c-.55 0-1-.45-1-1V3c0-.55.45-1 1-1h5.5l1 1 1-1H15c.55 0 1 .45 1 1zm-8 .5L7.5 3H2v9h6V3.5zm7-.5H9.5l-.5.5V12h6V3z" />
         </svg> README.md </h3> 
        <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p><a href="https://travis-ci.org/rrice/java-string-similarity"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/acc6850bc74d919d4e103152e26a29d80a8665b9/68747470733a2f2f7472617669732d63692e6f72672f72726963652f6a6176612d737472696e672d73696d696c61726974792e7376673f6272616e63683d6d6173746572" alt="Build Status" data-canonical-src="https://travis-ci.org/rrice/java-string-similarity.svg?branch=master" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p> 
         <p><a href="https://github.com/rrice/java-string-similarity">java-string-similarity</a> that calculates a normalized distance or similarity score between two strings. A score of 0.0 means that the two strings are absolutely dissimilar, and 1.0 means that absolutely similar (or equal). Anything in between indicates how similar each the two strings are.</p> 
         <h2><a id="user-content-example" class="anchor" href="#example" aria-hidden="true">
           <svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewbox="0 0 16 16" width="16">
            <path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path>
           </svg></a>Example</h2> 
         <p>In this simple example, we want to calculate a similarity score between the words <code>McDonalds</code> and <code>MacMahons</code>. We are selecting the <a href="http://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jaro%E2%80%93Winkler_distance">Jaro-Winkler distance algorithm</a> algorithm.</p> 
         <div class="highlight highlight-source-java">
          <pre><span class="pl-smi">SimilarityStrategy</span> strategy <span class="pl-k">=</span> <span class="pl-k">new</span> <span class="pl-smi">JaroWinklerStrategy</span>();
<span class="pl-smi">String</span> target <span class="pl-k">=</span> <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>McDonalds<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>;
<span class="pl-smi">String</span> source <span class="pl-k">=</span> <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>MacMahons<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>;
<span class="pl-smi">StringSimilarityService</span> service <span class="pl-k">=</span> <span class="pl-k">new</span> <span class="pl-smi">StringSimilarityServiceImpl</span>(strategy);
<span class="pl-k">double</span> score <span class="pl-k">=</span> service<span class="pl-k">.</span>score(source, target); <span class="pl-c"><span class="pl-c">//</span> Score is 0.90</span></pre>
         </div> 
         <h2><a id="user-content-algorithms" class="anchor" href="#algorithms" aria-hidden="true">
           <svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewbox="0 0 16 16" width="16">
            <path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path>
           </svg></a>Algorithms</h2> 
         <ul> 
          <li><a href="http://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jaro%E2%80%93Winkler_distance">Jaro distance</a></li>
