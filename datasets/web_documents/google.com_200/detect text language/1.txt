     <p><code>LanguageDetect</code> is a port of the <a href="http://pear.php.net/package/Text_LanguageDetect">PEAR::Text_LanguageDetect</a> for <a href="http://nodejs.org">node.js</a>.</p> 
     <p>LanguageDetect can identify 52 human languages from text samples and return confidence scores for each.</p>

     <ul>
