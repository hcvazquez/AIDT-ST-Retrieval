         <p>Most of the time, we are getting support questions of invalid configurations. We encourage everyone to have a look at our <a href="https://angular-translate.github.io/docs/#/guide">documentation website</a>. If you think the documentation is not correct (bug) or should be optimized (enhancement) please file an issue.</p> 
         <p>If you are still having difficulty after looking over your configuration carefully, please post a question to <a href="http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/angular-translate">StackOverflow with a specific tag</a>. Especially if the question are related to AngularJS or even JavaScript/browser basic technologies (maybe your issue is not related to angular-translate after all).</p> 
         <p>If you have discovered a bug or have a feature suggestion, feel free to create an issue on GitHub. Please follow the guideline within the issue template. See also next headline.</p> 
         <p><em>Please note: We cannot provide support for neither JavaScript nor AngularJS itself. In both cases, a platform like StackOverflow is much more ideal.</em></p>
