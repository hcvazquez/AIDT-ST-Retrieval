             <h3 class="post-title entry-title"> <a href="http://sourcesandmethods.blogspot.com.ar/2013/09/top-10-online-translation-services.html">Top 10 Free Online Translation Services </a> </h3> 
             <div class="post-header-line-1"></div> 
             <div class="post-body entry-content"> 
              <p><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>Ed. Note: &nbsp;Since last week's <a href="http://sourcesandmethods.blogspot.com/2013/09/top-11-online-language-learning.html">language learning resource blog post </a>was so well-received, consider this a second linguistically-inspired and - hopefully - equally well-received post (of which, I have no doubt, there will be many more).&nbsp;</i></span><br> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">The reality of the situation is that <a href="http://mkweb.bcgsc.ca/fun/globe/">this is what the world looks like</a>. We live in an increasingly multilingual society and, as analysts, this inarguably affects our jobs. Daily.&nbsp;</span><br> <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span> <br> </p>
              <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
               <tbody> 
                <tr>
                 <td style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ziTWhwTQWFs/UkNKvuVdp6I/AAAAAAAAASM/TpGd4_yNU5E/s1600/Langs.PNG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><span style="color: black; font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img border="0" height="355" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ziTWhwTQWFs/UkNKvuVdp6I/AAAAAAAAASM/TpGd4_yNU5E/s640/Langs.PNG" width="640"></span></a></td>
                </tr> 
                <tr>
                 <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: small;">Figure 1: Languages of the World - Source:&nbsp;<span style="background-color: white; text-align: start;"><a href="http://www.visualizing.org/visualizations/languages-world">This wonderful data visualization</a></span></span></td>
                </tr> 
               </tbody>
              </table> 
              <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"> So in light of this increasingly multilingual operating environment, how do we make our jobs easier?&nbsp;</span>
              <br> 
              <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br>Below is the answer (<i>an</i> answer, at least) to this question: A compendium of (free!) online machine translation services rank ordered by way of a little linguistics experiment!</span>
              <br> 
              <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span> 
              <br> 
              <div style="text-align: center;"> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><b>Top 10 Online Translation Services</b></span>
              </div> 
              <div style="text-align: center;"> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>(rank ordered by number of languages translated and evaluated on a five point scale in terms of error rate - 5/5 is best)</i></span>
              </div> 
              <div style="text-align: center;"> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><i><br></i></span>
              </div> 
              <div style="text-align: left;"> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">1.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Google Translate. 3/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">I would be remiss in this post if I did not include the infamous&nbsp;</span><a href="http://translate.google.com/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Google Translate</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;(for all the flack it gets, I really don't think they are&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">too</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;horrible). And besides, where else do you plan to translate from Azerbaijani to English? With 72 languages, they have the largest repository of the online translation services, but... then again, it's Google.</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">2.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Dictionary.com Translator. 3/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">The&nbsp;</span><a href="http://translate.reference.com/translate?query=transalte" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Dictionary.com translator</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;is a close second with 53 translation languages, Icelandic and Maltese among them.&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">The only downside is that there is a 300 character limit to translation so, if you're planning on dumping paragraphs into the translator (which you really shouldn't do anyway), this one is probably not for you.&nbsp;</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">3.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Bing</b>
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">.&nbsp;<b>3/5</b></span>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">The&nbsp;</span><a href="http://www.bing.com/translator" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Bing translator</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;maintains a 44 language repository with a user interface (UI) as appealing as Google's, and the recent buzzing in the blogosphere gives Bing the edge over Google when it comes to translation services.&nbsp;</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">4.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Free Translation. 3/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><a href="http://www.freetranslation.com/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Free Translation&nbsp;</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">(using SDL translation services) translates from 41 languages (Bengali among them!), but only into the big five (English, Spanish, Italian, French and Portugese).</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">5.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">BabelXl. 3/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><a href="http://babelxl.com/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Babelxl</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;translates 36 languages.</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">6. <b>itranslate4.eu</b></span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">.</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">This one is my personal favorite! As an avid linguist and frequent translator, I consider&nbsp;</span><a href="http://itranslate4.eu/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">itranslate4.eu</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;to be the most exciting find of the day. This site translates both in and out of 35 languages across many different search engines! Your output is the same translation provided by SYSTRAN, linguatec, PROMPT and others. This allows you to compare translations for greater comprehension and accuracy. Also, the language repository includes unexpected languages such as&nbsp;</span><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breton_language" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Breton</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;(go ahead ... look it up),&nbsp;</span><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esperanto#Classification" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Esperanto</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">,&nbsp;</span><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakh_language" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Kazakh</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;and&nbsp;</span><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Occitan_language" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Occitan</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">.</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">7.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Babylon. 3/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><a href="http://translation.babylon.com/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Babylon&nbsp;</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">translates 30, including two different forms of Chinese. It is very similar to the capabilities of&nbsp;</span><a href="http://www.worldlingo.com/en/products_services/worldlingo_translator.html" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Wordlingo</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">&nbsp;and&nbsp;</span><a href="http://translation.paralink.com/" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">imTranslator</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">.&nbsp;</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">8.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">SYSTRANet. 0/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">A little history: SYSTRAN is one of the oldest machine translation software (dating back to 1968) and, what do you know, they now have an online translator </span><a href="http://www.systranet.com/translate" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">SYSTRANet</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">. It translates out of 15 languages but only into the big 5. Google used SYSTRAN until circa 2007 and it is the current translation software behind the dashboard translator app for Mac OS X.&nbsp;</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">9.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Babelfish. 4/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><a href="http://www.babelfish.com/">Babelfish</a>&nbsp;(not to be confused with Yahoo! Babel Fish, which is now the Bing translator)&nbsp;translates 14 languages both ways.&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Again, the 300 character limit applies.</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">10.&nbsp;</span>
               <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Reverso. 2/5</b>
               <br> 
               <ul> 
                <li><a href="http://www.reverso.net/text_translation.aspx?lang=EN" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Reverso</a><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"> is an interesting site. It makes up for a clunky UI and limited language capacity (9 languages) with the option to have your full translation read to you in the target language simply by clicking a button.&nbsp;</span></li> 
               </ul> 
               <div style="text-align: center;"> 
                <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">A Linguistics Experiment</b>
               </div> 
               <div style="text-align: center;"> 
                <b style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><br></b>
               </div> 
               <div style="text-align: left;"> 
                <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">Just in case you were wondering, I didn't arbitrarily invent the number out of five (after all, who am I to judge; as I said before, I use Google Translate...)</span>
               </div> 
               <div style="text-align: left;"> 
                <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><br></span>
               </div> 
               <div style="text-align: left;"> 
                <div> 
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">In order to evaluate the 10 translation services listed above, I took a snippet from an article written in Spanish and plugged it in to all 10 translation engines. Below are the resulting ratings out of five calculated by subtracting the number of errors from five.</span>
                 <br> 
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><br></span>
                </div> 
                <div> 
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">This was a simple example and should in no way preclude you from trying all of these translation services at some point to determine your personal favorites, but based on this experiment, Babelfish returned the best translation.</span>
                 <br> 
                 <div> 
                  <blockquote class="tr_bq"> 
                   <i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><b>This is what was translated into English:</b>&nbsp;"</span><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="line-height: 18px;">Lingüistas de la Universidad de Glasgow han demostrado de que</span><span class="apple-converted-space" style="line-height: 18px;">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 18px;"><span style="border: 1pt none windowtext; padding: 0in;">ver la televisión de manera activa puede cambiar rápidamente un determinado acento</span></span><span style="line-height: 18px;">. Tal y como publican en la revista especializada Language, sus conclusiones se basan en el análisis de los efectos de la telenovela británica</span><span class="apple-converted-space" style="line-height: 18px;">&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 18px;"><span style="border: 1pt none windowtext; padding: 0in;">EastEnders</span></span><span style="line-height: 18px;">, emitida por la cadena británica BBC, sobre el modo de hablar de los escoceses."</span></span></i>
                  </blockquote> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="line-height: 18px;"><b>Common errors included:</b></span></span>
                  <br> 
                  <ul> 
                   <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Failing to translate the infinitive Spanish verb "</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">ver"</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">&nbsp;into the gerund English equivalent&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">watching</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">&nbsp;(as opposed to&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">watch</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">).&nbsp;</span></li> 
                   <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Incorrect syntactical word order of adverbs in the first sentence such as&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">actively&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">("</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">de manera activa")&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">and&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">quickly&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">("</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">rapidamente</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">").&nbsp;</span></li> 
                   <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">No service except for Google Translate managed to successfully translate "</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">revista especializada"&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">to&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">journal</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">&nbsp;as opposed to&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">specialized magazine.</i></li> 
                   <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">Mistranslation of the past participle&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">"emitida"&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="line-height: 18px;">to various other English connotations such as&nbsp;<i>emitted</i>&nbsp;or&nbsp;</span></span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">issued.</i></li> 
                   <li><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">No service successfully translated the final clause&nbsp;</span><i style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">"sobre el modo de hablar de los escoceces,"&nbsp;</i><span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; line-height: 18px;">which means "about the Scottish way of speaking" or, more colloquially, "about the Scottish accent." Common translations were: "<i>the way to speak of the Scotts," "the way of speaking about the Scotts," and "how to talk about the Scotts."&nbsp;</i>This is because in this clause,&nbsp;<i>"de"&nbsp;</i>can be interpreted to mean either "<i>of"&nbsp;</i>(correct) or&nbsp;<i>"about"&nbsp;</i>(incorrect); so this error was due to a mistranslation of the preposition.</span></li> 
                  </ul> 
                 </div> 
                 <div> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><i>**Note: Most U.S.-based translation services translate English-Spanish better than other languages because that is the language combination historically in the most demand within the United States. In short, the more obscure the language, the less accurate the translation. This translation experiment is probably a best case scenario.</i></span>
                 </div> 
                 <div> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><i><br></i></span>
                 </div> 
                 <div> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">For those interested in reading the article from which the sample text came, it is from the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.muyinteresante.es/">Muy Interesante</a>&nbsp;(the&nbsp;<i>New Science Magazine&nbsp;</i>of Latin America) titled&nbsp;<i><a href="http://www.muyinteresante.es/cultura/arte-cultura/articulo/ver-la-television-puede-cambiar-tu-acento-511378898907">Ver la television puede cambiar tu acento!</a>&nbsp;</i>(Watching TV Can Change Your Accent).&nbsp;</span>
                 </div> 
                 <div> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;"><br></span>
                 </div> 
                 <div> 
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Don't speak Spanish? Use one of your&nbsp;new-found&nbsp;translation services!</span>
