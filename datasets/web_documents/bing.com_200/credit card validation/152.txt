            <br> 
            <br> Check this card number: 5368235896831135
            <br> 
            <br> 
            <b>Step 1</b>: Starting from the second digit from the right and moving towards the left, multiply every digit by 2.
            <br> 
            <br> 5368235896831135
            <br> ^ ^ ^ ^ ^ ^ ^ ^
            <br> 
            <br> 3*2=6
            <br> 1*2=2
            <br> 8*2=16
            <br> 9*2=18
            <br> 5*2=10
            <br> 2*2=4
            <br> 6*2=12
            <br> 5*2=10
            <br> 
            <br> 
            <b>Step 2</b>: Sum the digits from Step 1, if the value is bigger than 9, we must add 2 digits together. Ex : 16 = 1+6 = 7
            <br> 
            <br> So we have : 6 + 2 + (1+6) + (1+8) + (1+0) + 4 + (1+2) + (1+0) = 33
            <br> 
            <b><br> Step 3</b>: Sum all of the digits not originally multiplied by 2 (without begining number from right: 5)
            <br> 
            <br> So we have: 3+8+3+8+6+3+1=32
            <br> 
            <br> 
            <b>Step 4</b>: Sum together the results from Step 2 and Step 3.
            <br> 
            <br> 33 + 32 = 65
            <br> 
            <b><br> Step 5</b>: Subtract the sum from the next highest multiple of 10
            <br> 
            <br> 
            <br> 70 - 65 = 5 [check digit]
            <br>
