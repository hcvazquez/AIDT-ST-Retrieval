               <h1>18 WordPress Calculator Plugins to Engage Your Visitors</h1> 
              </div> 
              <div class="wpmud-entry__before-content"></div> 
              <div class="wpmud-entry__content"> 
               <p><a rel="lightbox[36350]" class="blog-thumbnail" href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/calculator.png"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-36372" title="calculator" src="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/calculator.png" alt="" width="128" height="128"></a>Calculators are often overlooked as a simple utility, but you might be surprised by how much they increase user interaction on your site. If a user finds your calculator extremely useful, he’s likely to bookmark your site so that he doesn’t have to search for it the next time. You may not be aware of the variety of calculator plugins available for WordPress, so here’s an excellent sampling of recently updated plugins that will perform calculations on your website.</p> 
               <p>If your site depends on being able to provide some sophisticated calculations for your visitors, then WordPress has the calculator for you. Many of these can be used in combination with a BuddyPress site to promote a community that’s based on mutual interests and goals, such as running, losing weight, or getting out of debt.</p> 
               <h2>Mortgage Calculator Sidebar Widget</h2> 
               <p><a rel="lightbox[36350]" class="blog-thumbnail" href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/folder_home.png"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-36395" title="folder_home" src="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/folder_home.png" alt="" width="128" height="128"></a>A Mortgage Calculator sidebar widget that enables your visitors to calculate their monthly payments towards their mortgage.</p> 
               <h3><a href="http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/mortgage-calculator-sidebar-widget/" target="_blank">Download Plugin</a></h3> 
               <h2 style="padding-top: 18px; clear: both;">Mortgage Loan Calculator</h2> 
               <p><a rel="lightbox[36350]" class="blog-thumbnail" href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/mortgagepayment.png"><img class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-36359" title="mortgagepayment" src="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/mortgagepayment-150x150.png" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a>This is a sidebar widget that calculates your principal and interest mortgage loan payment. You insert the loan amount and interest rate (pop up tells you to insert a number if you put anything other than a number in), choose the length of the loan from a drop-down, and press the calculate button. Your payment information is displayed.</p> 
               <h3><a href="http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/simple-mortgage-loan-calculator-3/" target="_blank">Download Plugin</a></h3> 
               <h2 style="padding-top: 18px; clear: both;">Financial Toolbox</h2> 
               <p><a rel="lightbox[36350]" class="blog-thumbnail" href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/toolbox_folder_badged.png"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-36396" title="toolbox_folder_badged" src="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/toolbox_folder_badged.png" alt="" width="128" height="128"></a>The financial toolbox includes a <a href="https://premium.wpmudev.org/blog/5-wordpress-scrolling-ticker-plugins-to-liven-up-your-site/">news ticker</a> for the latest financial news along with an integrated credit calculator.</p>

You can mark any asset or liability value as private if you do not want it to displayed.</p>
