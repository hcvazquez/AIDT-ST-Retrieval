                  <div class="posttext">
                    If a customer does not specifically select the Credit Card Expiration Date and leaves it as the default (01/Current Year), then the result on Confirmation of the order is an error saying their credit card is expired.
                   <br>
                   <br>This may be counter-intuitive for most people as they have no clue that they neglected to change the Expiration date on the Payment info (it happens).&nbsp;&nbsp;I got a very nasty email telling me I needed to "fix my store" and lost a $50 order.&nbsp;&nbsp;After testing it, I discovered she left the expiration date the default.
                   <br>
                   <br>I would suggest defaulting the Month and Year to -- / ---- and validate off that to force the user to enter a date.
