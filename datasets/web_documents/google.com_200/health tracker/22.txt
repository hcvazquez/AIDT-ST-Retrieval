       <h2 class="mainBody">Employee Health &amp; Wellness</h2> 
       <div id="leftColumn">
        <p>The Employee Wellness &amp; Health Promotion Program is a vital<strong> </strong>part of the healthcare package offered to Pacific Union College employees. The goals of the Wellness Program are to offer information and opportunities to support employees’ health and vitality, support a healthy work environment for all. Spouses and employees who are insured elsewhere are also encouraged to take advantage of many of the benefits of the Wellness program. </p> 
        <p><a class="linkColor" href="http://wellness.puc.edu"><strong>Click this Link to LOG IN to your personal Wellness Pages, to “Start a New Assessment” and/or access the “Health Activity Tracker”</strong></a></p> 
        <h3 class="mainSubHead">Your PUC Healthcare Plan Contribution + Wellness Participation = $REBATE$</h3> 
        <p> Your Healthcare Plan contributions fund about 10%-15% of PUC’s self-insured healthcare plan account.&nbsp; PUC covers the rest, with costs rising and falling each year depending on how healthy we are. Participation in the Wellness Program is rewarded with a rebate of a portion of your PUC healthcare plan when both you and your PUC Healthcare Plan-covered spouse participate.&nbsp; To be eligible for the rebate, you and your PUC Healthcare Plan-covered spouse will complete the following: &nbsp;</p> 
        <ol> 
         <li>Annual <strong>P</strong>ersonal <strong>W</strong>ellness <strong>P</strong>rofile (<strong>PWP</strong>) online assessment</li> 
         <li>Annual <strong>W</strong>ellbeing<strong> S</strong>creening of Biometrics* and Physical Fitness levels</li> 
         <li><strong>H</strong>ealth <strong>A</strong>ctivity <strong>T</strong>racking through one of the following two options: 
          <ul> 
           <li>&nbsp;Accumulate 500 credits/points each 6-month period using the online “<strong>H</strong>ealth <strong>A</strong>ctivity <strong>T</strong>racker” (<strong>HAT</strong>). &nbsp;The <strong>HAT</strong> and <strong>PWP</strong> are accessed through <a href="http://wellness.puc.edu">wellness.puc.edu</a>.</li> 
           <li>Track your healthy activities using an online or smartphone application of your choice.&nbsp; Send an email to <a href="mailto:wellness@puc.edu">wellness@puc.edu</a> of a <strong>screenshot</strong> of at least <strong>two months of active tracking</strong> during the current period from your chosen application/tracking method.&nbsp; </li> 
          </ul> </li> 
        </ol> 
        <p>? <strong><a class="linkColor" href="http://wellness.puc.edu">Click this link to access the "Health Activity Tracker" to log in your Wellness Credits, and/or to "Start a New Assessment" under the Personal Wellness Profile tab</a></strong></p> 
        <h3 class="mainSubHead">Program Timeline</h3> 
        <p><strong>Complete the annual in-office screening <u>and</u> online assessment in either period 1 or 2 and within 10 to 14 months of your last screening to remain in the program.</strong></p> 
        <ul type="disc"> 
         <li><strong>July 1&nbsp; to June 30 – </strong>Complete the annual Wellness Biometrics &amp; Fitness Screening<strong> </strong>plus the Personal Wellness Profile within 10-14 months of last year’s assessment or within 6 months of start of employment.</li> 
         <li><strong>July 1 – December 30</strong> - Period 1 Activity Tracking: Log your points regularly on the HAT</li> 
         <li><strong>December 30</strong> - Deadline to log 500 pts on HAT or email your tracker app screenshots</li> 
         <li><strong>January</strong> - <u>Rebate through payroll after January 15</u></li> 
         <li><strong>January 1 – June 30</strong> - Period 2 Activity Tracking: Log your points regularly on the HAT</li> 
         <li><strong>June 30</strong> - Deadline to log 500 pts on HAT or email your tracker app screenshots</li> 
         <li><strong>July</strong> - <u>Rebate through payroll after July 15</u></li> 
        </ul> 
       </div>
