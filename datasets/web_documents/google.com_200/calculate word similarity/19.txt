       <h1 id="rn_Summary">How long does it take to generate the similarity report in Turnitin?</h1> 
      </div> 
      <div id="rn_PageContent" class="rn_AnswerDetail"> 
       <div id="rn_AnswerText"> 
        <p>Please allow <span style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold">up to 24 hours</span> for a report to be generated for your first submission. Resubmitted papers will take a <span style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold">minimum of 24 hours</span> to generate a new&nbsp;similarity report for each submission.</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>If the similarity index is still showing "pending', try selecting the “Refresh Submissions” link under “My Submissions” tab to reload the page&nbsp; If the report is still not available, please now try opening up your file in the Turnitin Document Viewer. After you click on your file it will open in a new tab - your&nbsp;Similarity Report may be available here, even when it is still showing as 'Pending' on your my submissions page</li> 
         <li>If you have resubmitted an assignment, there&nbsp;will be a delay of 24 hours before an originality report is available</li> 
         <li>The course convenor can choose whether to allow students to view the&nbsp;similarity report</li> 
         <li>Standard practice is to allow students to view the report for Draft submissions, but generally not for Final submissions</li> 
         <li>If you are unsure if you should be able to see a Similarity&nbsp;Report, please check instructions in your Course site, or contact your Convenor</li> 
        </ul> 
        <p>If the&nbsp;Similarity Report for your first submission to a DRAFT is still pending after 24 Hours, <span style="TEXT-DECORATION: underline">do not resubmit</span> as reports for resubmissions will always take 24 hours to generate. Instead, please try the 'Refresh submissions' and checking inside the Turnitin Document Viewer steps&nbsp;mentioned at the top of this page. If&nbsp;you have tried these&nbsp;two steps and the Report is still not available, please contact the IT Service Centre on 3735 5555.</p> 
        <p>For more information, see</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><a style="TEXT-DECORATION: underline" href="https://intranet.secure.griffith.edu.au/computing/using-learning-at-griffith/students/assessment/turnitin">Turnitin</a></li> 
         <li><a style="TEXT-DECORATION: underline" href="https://www.griffith.edu.au/information-services/service-catalogue/it-service-centre">IT Service Centre</a></li> 
        </ul> 
       </div>
