                       <li><span class="bold">No more retyping</span><br>If you lose or accidentally erase an important digital file, such as a proposal or invoice, but still have a hard copy, you can easily replace it in your digital filing system by using OCR software to scan the paper original or most recent draft. </li> 
                       <li><span class="bold">Quick digital searches</span><br>OCR software converts scanned text into a word processing file, giving you the opportunity to search for specific documents using a keyword or phrase.                        <li><span class="bold">Edit text</span><br>Once you’ve scanned your document using OCR, you have the option to edit the text within a word processing program of your choice. You can easily turn a filing cabinet worth of information into editable digital files, and create a backup system consisting of a single CD. </li> 
                       <li><span class="bold">Accessibility</span><br>OCR software is a useful Accessibility or Ease of Access tool. Vision impaired PC users can scan books, magazines, incoming faxes, or other documents into word processing programs to be used in conjunction with a computer voice-over utility. </li>
