           <p class="mgbot_10">SCORM Player is a lightweight application that enables you to import SCORM packages and read their content within a user-friendly, simplistic interface. It is the tool you need for viewing e-learning content, providing you with a simple program that can be used for exploring the package structure.</p> 
           <h4>Cross-platform support and ease of use</h4> 
           <p class="mgbot_10">Built using <a href="http://www.softpedia.com/get/System/OS-Enhancements/Adobe-AIR.shtml" target="_blank">Adobe AIR</a>, this application is compatible with all the popular platforms. Focusing on simplicity and ease of use, it features a forthright interface that comprises all the options within a single, minimalistic window, with no menus and advanced options whatsoever. Your task is very simple: in order to open a package, you must press the 'Select File' button and browse for its location.</p> 
           <p class="mgbot_10">The GUI is split into two separate panes, one for navigating throughout the course structure and chapters and the other for reading the text. For your convenience, the structure of the SCORM file is displayed in tree view.</p> 
           <h4>Content reading and navigation options</h4> 
           <p class="mgbot_10">There are some options designed to facilitate content exploring, such as the navigation buttons. You can use them to go further or backwards within the course, quickly jumping to another page. In addition to this, SCORM Player allows full screen display, which allow you to view the displayed content on the entire screen.</p> 
           <p class="mgbot_10">Please note that this application only shows you the content of a SCORM course and cannot act as an editor or package creator. Therefore, third party applications are required in order to create e-learning content, quizzes and surveys.</p> 
           <h4>A worth-having SCORM reading tool</h4> 
           <p class="mgbot_10">SCORM Player is intended for those who are working with a complete SCORM solution for their learning management system. It enables you to play SCORM content on your computer, without having to deal with complicated options.</p>
