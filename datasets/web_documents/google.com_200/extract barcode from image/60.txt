    <h3 class="feature-description">Feature Description</h3> 
    <p class="introParagraph"> LEADTOOLS Driver's License recognition and processing SDK technology provides .NET (C# &amp; VB) and web developers with a high-level framework to classify and extract data from driver's licenses and similar forms of identification. The state-of-the-art recognition algorithms is capable of recognizing structured and unstructured driving licenses and ID cards from any country, state, or agency. </p> 
    <p class="introParagraph"> Programmers requiring unique, accurate, and efficient methods of customer data capture will find great value in LEADTOOLS Driver's License recognition. In addition to benefiting from the world-class recognition accuracy of LEADTOOLS, the toolkit includes customizable master templates for all fifty states in the US and Washington DC, which will significantly jump-start development and reduce the time required to create and test licenses for their application. Moreover, an implementation to recognize and process unstructured ID cards is included. </p> 
    <p class="demoLink"><a href="https://demo.leadtools.com/JavaScript/DriverLicenseFormsRecognition/" target="_blank">Free Live Online Demo - Click here to see LEADTOOLS Driver's License Processing in action</a></p> 
    <h2>Overview of LEADTOOLS Driver's License Recognition and Processing SDK Technology</h2> 
    <ul> 
     <li> Find and extract text, numeric, and date information from any driver's license or identification card: 
      <ul> 
       <li>Name</li> 
       <li>ID Number</li> 
       <li>Address</li> 
       <li>Gender</li> 
       <li>Race</li> 
       <li>Organ donor election</li> 
       <li>Signature</li> 
       <li>Date of Birth</li> 
       <li>Issuing Date</li> 
       <li>Expiration Date</li> 
      </ul> </li> 
     <li>Provides demo implementation to recognize and process unstructured ID cards (i.e., unstructured forms). No template is required</li> 
     <li> Finds fields with common headers such as name, address, date of birth, issuing date, expiration date, and ID number, even when the ID does not match any template </li> 
     <li> Includes master templates for all fifty states in the US and Washington DC: templates are easily customizable to support any country, state, or agency with standardized identification cards </li> 
     <li>Recognize and extract driver's license fields regardless of image resolution, scale, and other image generation characteristics</li> 
     <li>Advanced scanned image alignment algorithm compensates for non-linear deformations introduced by different scanners, printers, and resolutions</li> 
     <li>World-class accuracy and speed</li> 
     <li>Unique color and bitonal image recognition for scanned documents and pictures</li> 
     <li>Automatic detection and correction of driver's license orientation and skew angle</li> 
     <li>Utilize camera preview on phones and tablets for real-time driver's license recognition and processing</li> 
     <li>Enhance recognition accuracy with powerful <a href="/sdk/image-processing/document">document cleanup</a> and preprocessing</li> 
     <li>Use regular expressions to find and validate text</li> 
     <li> Comprehensive confidence reporting for each driver's license field type: 
      <ul> 
       <li>Character location, size, and baseline</li> 
       <li>Character attributes (end of word, end of line, end of paragraph, etc.)</li> 
       <li>Font properties (monospace, proportional, serif, sans-serif, bold, italic, underline, strikethrough)</li> 
       <li>Confidence values</li> 
      </ul> </li> 
     <li>Load and save master template identification information as XML for simplified storage and editing</li> 
     <li>Utilize <a href="/sdk/twain/fast-twain">LEADTOOLS Fast Twain</a> to rapidly scan driving licenses and ID cards</li> 
     <li>Demos with source code provide complete implementation for live capture, recognition, and processing of ID cards</li> 
     <li>Native 32 and 64-bit driver's license recognition and processing binaries</li> 
    </ul>
