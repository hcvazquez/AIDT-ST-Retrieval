            <div class="Header_1">
             <span class="">Interpreter/Translation Providers</span>
            </div>
            <br>
            <div>
             <a name="Skip"></a>
            </div>
            <ul style="undefined">
             <li style="undefined"><a href="#foreignlanguage">Foreign language customers</a></li>
             <li style="undefined"><a href="#deafhard">Deaf/Hard of Hearing Customers</a></li>
             <li style="undefined"><a href="#processseeking">Application Process for Individuals Seeking Placement on the MVA's Approved Interpreter/Translator Provider Listing</a></li>
            </ul>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Header_2">Foreign Language Customers<a name="foreignlanguage"></a></span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">If you have difficulty communicating in English or are deaf/severely hearing impaired, you may be able to use an interpreter when you visit the MVA, particularly when taking a knowledge test skills driving test for a class C or M non-commercial driver’s license.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">The MVA’s law test system offers the Non-Commercial Class C test in the following languages:</span></p>
            <ul>
             <li><p>English</p></li>
             <li><p>Spanish</p></li>
             <li><p>French</p></li>
             <li><p>Korean</p></li>
             <li><p>Traditional Chinese</p></li>
             <li><p>Vietnamese&nbsp;</p></li>
            </ul>
            <p><u>If we offer the knowledge test in the language that you speak, you must attempt the knowledge test at least <strong>five (5)</strong>&nbsp;times before an interpreter can be used.</u></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">If your identity documents and/or driver’s license is not in English, the document(s) must be translated by an MVA approved Interpreter/Translator. A foreign issued license does not need to be translated if accompanied by an international driver's license.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><strong>Interpreter/Translation Providers Accepted by the MVA</strong></span></p>
            <ul style="undefined">
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><a title="MVA Approved Interpreter/Translator Listing" href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/_resources/docs/drivers/int-trans-list.pdf">MVA's Approved Interpreter/Translator Provider Listing</a>; <strong>OR</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><a title="MVA's State of Maryland Court Interpreter Registry Listing" href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/_resources/docs/Interpreter-List.pdf">MVA's State of Maryland Court Interpreter Registry Listing</a>; <strong>OR</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><a href="http://www.state.gov/s/cpr/rls/dpl/32122.htm">The Embassy</a> of the issuing document's country of origin (may only be used for the translation of documents); <strong>OR</strong></span></li>
            </ul>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">&nbsp;</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><strong>NOTE:</strong> The MVA does not require a fee for the use of an interpreter; however, you will be responsible for any fees that the interpreter may require for their services.<br> &nbsp;</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal"><strong>Interpreter/Translation Requirements</strong></span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; In-person interpreter and/or translator providers must show a valid photo identification card (either a state driver's license or identification card) prior to conducting on-site interpretation and/or translation services.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; MVA branch office staff will check the MVA's approved Interpreter/Translator lists to confirm current standing.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Interpreters providing services for knowledge/skills testing are prohibited from providing other services on behalf of the customer being tested, such as:</span></p>
            <p style="margin-left: 40px"><span class="Normal">o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span> Certifying residence,</p>
            <p style="margin-left: 40px"><span class="Normal">o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Conducting the business of a driving school,</span></p>
            <p style="margin-left: 40px"><span class="Normal">o&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Issuing lease/rental agreements, etc.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<strong>Documents</strong>: The original document must accompany the English translation.</span></p>
            <p style="margin-left: 18.75pt"><span class="Normal">Document Translations must be:</span></p>
            <ul style="margin-left: 80px">
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">On a document prepared by an MVA approved provider or court translator; <strong>OR</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">On the letterhead of the embassy of the country of origin; <strong>OR</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">On the letterhead of the translator contracted by the State.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p style="margin-left: 18.75pt"><span class="Normal">All of the above documents must contain the:</span></p>
            <ul style="margin-left: 80px">
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Typed/printed name of the translator; <strong>AND</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Telephone number; <strong>AND</strong></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Signature of translator.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p style="undefined"><br> <span class="Normal">5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Interpreters cannot provide interpretation services for family members.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined">6. &nbsp; &nbsp;Interpreters are prohibited from soliciting on MVA property.</p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">7.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; You <strong>cannot</strong> use an interpreter when taking a CDL knowledge test or skills test for a commercial driver’s license (CDL) or Class A or B noncommercial.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Header_2">Deaf/Hard of Hearing Customers<a name="deafhard"></a></span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Contact the <a href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/about-mva/info/26300/26300-40T.htm">TTY/Hard of Hearing number</a> to ask for the assistance of a sign language interpreter for such transactions as:</span></p>
            <p style="margin-left: 18.75pt"><span class="Normal">1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Noncommercial Driver licensing knowledge&nbsp;and&nbsp;skills&nbsp;tests</span></p>
            <p style="margin-left: 18.75pt"><span class="Normal">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Medical Advisory Board Interviews</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Once you call the TTY/Hearing Impaired number, or MVA’s Customer Service Center 410-768-7000, the MVA’s Driver Services Division will:</span></p>
            <ul style="margin-left: 40px">
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Make all the arrangements;</span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Pay for the interpreter;</span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Ask you for the date, time and location of the branch office where you plan to visit;</span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Provide you with confirmation of your appointment once arrangements have been made.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">&nbsp;</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">Please contact our Customer Service Center at 410-768-7000 if you have any other questions regarding the assistance of a sign language interpreter.</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Header_2">Application Process for Individuals Seeking Placement on the MVA's Approved Interpreter/Translator Provider Listing<a name="processseeking"></a></span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">The MVA is currently accepting applications from individuals interested in applying for placement on the <a title="MVA Approved Interpreter/Translator Listing" href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/_resources/docs/drivers/int-trans-list.pdf">MVA's Approved Interpreter/Translator Provider Listing</a>. A new listing is released on a quarterly basis.&nbsp;</span></p>
            <p style="undefined"><span class="Normal">If you are interested in applying for placement on the MVA's Approved Interpreter/Translator Provider Listing, you must complete:</span></p>
            <ol start="1" style="undefined">
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">MVA's <a href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/_resources/docs/DL-200.pdf">Application to Provide Interpretation and/or Translation Services (form DL-200)</a></span></li>
             <li style="undefined"><span class="Normal">The <a href="http://www.mva.maryland.gov/_resources/docs/DL-201.pdf">Interpreter and/or Translator Certification Form (form&nbsp;DL-201)</a>;</span></li>
