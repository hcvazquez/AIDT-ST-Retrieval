   <div id="quickSummary">
    <p>LilyPond is a music engraving program, devoted to producing the highest-quality sheet music possible. It brings the aesthetics of traditionally engraved music to computer printouts. LilyPond is free software and part of the <a href="http://gnu.org">GNU Project</a>. </p> 
    <div class="align-right">
     <p>Read more in our <a href="introduction.html">Introduction</a>! </p>

     <p>LilyPond is a powerful and flexible tool for engraving tasks of all kinds, for example <a href="examples.html#Classical-Music">classical music</a> (like the example above by J.S. Bach), <a href="examples.html#Complex-Notation">complex notation</a>, <a href="examples.html#Early-Music">early music</a>, <a href="examples.html#Modern-Music">modern music</a>, <a href="examples.html#Tablature">tablature</a>, <a href="examples.html#Vocal-Music">vocal music</a>, <a href="examples.html#Lead-Sheets">lead sheets</a>, <a href="examples.html#Educational-Applications">educational materials</a>, <a href="examples.html#Large-Projects">large orchestral projects</a>, <a href="examples.html#Customized-Output">customized output</a>, and even <a href="examples.html#Schenker-Graphs">Schenker graphs</a>. </p> 
     <p>Browse our gallery of <a href="examples.html">Examples</a> and be inspired! </p>
