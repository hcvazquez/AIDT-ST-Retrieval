           <div itemprop="commentText" class="post entry-content ">
             It works!
            <br>
            <br>from the package, you'll need:
            <br>
            <br>
            <em class="bbc">bmptopnm.exe<br>pnmscale.exe<br>pnmtopng.exe</em>
            <br>
            <br>and in the same folder:
            <br>
            <br>
            <em class="bbc">libnetpbm10.dll<br>libpng12.dll<br>zlib1.dll</em>
            <br>
            <br>with this command line:
            <br>
            <br>
            <span style="color: blue">bmptopnm input.bmp | pnmscale -xscale=0.5 -yscale=0.5 | pnmtopng &gt; output.png</span>
            <br>
            <br>you'll get a png from a bmp which is half the size (dimensions) as the original...
            <br>
            <br> 8)
