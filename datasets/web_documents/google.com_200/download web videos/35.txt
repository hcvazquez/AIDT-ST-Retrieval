     <h2>Media Support</h2> 
     <ul class="short"> 
      <li><strong>HTML5:</strong> mp3, mp4 (AAC/H.264), ogg (Vorbis/Theora), webm (Vorbis/VP8), wav</li> 
      <li><strong>Flash:</strong> mp3, mp4 (AAC/H.264), rtmp, flv</li> 
     </ul> 
     <p class="note" style="margin-top:10px;"> For cross-browser support, a format must be supplied that works in both HTML5 and Flash.<br> Optional additional formats may be supplied to increase cross-browser HTML5 support. </p>
