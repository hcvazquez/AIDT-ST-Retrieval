      <p>You can't just download torrents like you do other types of files. Instead, you need a dedicated torrent downloader, a special kind of program that downloads the actual movie and music files that the <a href="https://www.lifewire.com/torrent-file-2622839" data-component="link" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" data-ordinal="1">torrent file</a> is pointing to.</p>
      <p>You have two broad choices when it comes to torrent downloading tools:&nbsp;<em>cloud software</em>, which are online services that you subscribe to; and <em>client software</em>, which are programs that you install directly on your computer. Of course, always be sure you're running fully updated antimalware software before you visit any of these sites.</p>
      <p>While cloud software is arguably better at cloaking your P2P habits, both cloud and client software have their advantages. Each offer different levels of price, control, and customization to suit different tastes.</p>
