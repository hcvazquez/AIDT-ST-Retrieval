             <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
              <p>I am using Word2Vec for text vectorization. It is doing a good job but some cases it is failing. For example "turn the computer off and on" and the sentence "restart the computer" does not have a very good similarity score, even though they mean the same thing. Doc2Vec is not doing a good job as my inputs are usually a couple of sentences and not a document.</p> 
              <p>Can anyone please suggest an approach which would give a good similarity score between "turn on and off" and "restart" and also other combinations like that?</p>
