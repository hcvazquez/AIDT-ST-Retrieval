function addEvent(obj, evType, fn){
    if (obj.addEventListener){
        obj.addEventListener(evType, fn, false);
        return true;
    } else if (obj.attachEvent){
        var r = obj.attachEvent("on"+evType, fn);
        return r;
    } else {
        return false;
    }
}

              <h3 class="contentheader">Getting Started</h3> 
              <div id="SIGNUPVBOXACCOUNT" class="signupvboxaccount headlinespace"> 
               <p class="linespace"><strong>Sign up for your SAM account</strong></p> 
               <p class="subhead"> To sign up, simply visit "<a href="/process/dmbox/reg" class="hyperlink-color">Sign up</a>" page.</p> 
               <ul style="padding-left: 15px;"> 
                <li>Please fill up the registration form.</li> 
                <li>Please ensure that all the mandatory fields (indicated with asterisks "*") are entered.</li> 
               </ul>

              <div id="VERIFYING_YOUR_VBOXACCOUNT" class="verifyingyourvboxaccount headlinespace"> 
               <p class="linespace"><strong>Verifying your SAM account</strong></p> 
               <p> As SAM works in a closed and trusted system, whereby only known senders are allowed to send mail to verified users. This verification process serves to provide assurance regarding the identity of the users who sign up for our digital mailbox. As part of the verification process, you will be required to submit a copy of your identification document. </p> 
               <p> For Singaporean and Permanent Residents, please submit a copy of the front and back of your NRIC.For foreigners, please submit a copy of your permit or passport. If your identification document does not include your mailing address, you will also be required to submit a copy of your latest utility or telecommunication bills that clearly states your mailing address. </p> 
               <p> These are the different ways you can submit your identification document: </p> 
               <ol style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                <li><strong><em>Upload Identification Document</em></strong></li> 
                <ol type="a" style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                 <li>Login to SAM with your SingPost ID and password. If your account is not verified, you will be prompted to perform verification.</li> 
                 <li>From the verification prompt, choose "Upload Identification Document". </li> 
                 <li>Click on the Browse button to select your Identification Document. Then click on Upload.</li> 
                </ol> 
                <li><strong><em>Email Identification Document</em></strong></li> 
                <ol type="a" style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                 <li>You may also wish to submit your identification document by email to <a href="mailto:support@mysam.sg">support@mysam.sg</a>.</li> 
                 <li>Please remember to indicate your SingPost ID in the email.</li> 
                </ol> 
                <li><strong><em>Submit Identification Document through Post Office</em></strong></li> 
                <ol type="a" style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                 <li>You can also submit your identification document by making a trip to our Post Office network.</li> 
                 <li>Simply inform our counter staff that you are submitting your identification document for SAM account verification.</li> 
                 <li>Do remember to inform our counter staff what is your SingPost ID.</li> 
                </ol> 
               </ol> 
               <p> Once we have checked your identification document, we will mail your "Verification PIN" to your registered address within the next 3-5 working days. When you receive the "Verification PIN", please login to SAM with your SingPost ID and password. You will be prompted to enter your "Verification PIN" that is specified in the letter. Simply fill in the correct "Verification PIN" and you will be verified. </p> 
              </div> 
              <div id="SUBSCRIBING_YOUR_MAIL" class="subscribeyourmail headlinespace"> 
               <p class="linespace"><strong>Subscribing your mail</strong></p> 
               <p>You may subscribe to your mail from via Connections &gt; Mail Subscriptions for senders who are onboard SAM (represented by their logos). You will be able to view the different services that the senders have by clicking on their logo. Simply click on the service, enter your account details with the sender and select the types of mail you wish to subscribe from the sender. </p>

               <h3 class="contentheader">Using SAM</h3> 
               <div id="PAYMENT" class="payment"> 
                <p class="linespace" style="padding-top:10px"> <strong>How can I qualify to use this service?</strong><br> You need to be a verified SAM member. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>What are the available modes of payment for Bill Payment Service?</strong><br> Currently, the following are the available modes of payment:</p> 
                <ol type="a" style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                 <li>GIRO-On-Demand (GOOD)</li> 
                 <li>eNETS Debit</li> 
                 <li>Credit/Debit Cards (Visa/MasterCard)</li> 
                </ol> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Can I use a corporate or foreign credit card? </strong><br> SAM currently only accepts locally issued credit cards for payments. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Do all the Billers accept all the available modes of payment?</strong><br> Depending on the billers, they may not support all our modes of payments. The payment modes the biller supports will be shown when you make the payment to the biller. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How can I pay my bills if my service provider is not listed in the list of billers under "Select Biller" payment?</strong><br> At the moment, SAM only allows you to pay your bill from your mail directly or if your service provider is listed in the list of billers under "Others" payment. If you wish for any service providers to present your digital mail or pay your bills in SAM, you may inform your service provider directly or inform us through our Contact Us page. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Can I make partial payments?</strong><br> Yes, you can control the payment amount and timing of your payment. However, please remember to pay your bill in full by the bill due date, as relevant late payment charges may be imposed by various billers on your account. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>What is GIRO-On-Demand?</strong><br> The GIRO-On-Demand is slightly different from the current GIRO service. For the current GIRO service, the amount due is automatically deducted from your bank account on a fixed day of the month without your intervention. On the other hand, the GIRO-On-Demand service, offers you better control as the amount due will only be deducted from your bank account after you have specifically initiated the deduction instruction on the SAM website. You will have to instruct your bank to allow you to pay on the SAM website. The Application for GIRO-On-Demand form will also authorize SingPost to instruct your bank to pay your billers once you have activated the "Pay by GIRO-On-Demand" instruction via your SAM account </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Must I sign up for GIRO-On-Demand for SAM payment service?</strong><br> It is not compulsory as GIRO-On-Demand is just another way to pay your bills online at SAM. You may use other modes of payment available for the respective billers. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How do I apply for a GIRO-on-Demand account?</strong><br> To apply for GIRO-On-Demand account, simply follow these steps: </p>
                <ol style="padding-left: 5px;"> 
                 <li>Download the application form.<a href="javascript:downloadForm();">(Click here)</a></li> 
                 <li>Print out the form and complete section 1.</li> 
                 <li>Post the completed form to:<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Digital Services<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;10 Eunos Road 8, #07-31<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Singapore Post Centre<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Singapore 408600</li> 
                 <li>The form will then be forwarded to your bank for approval. The bank will typically take approximately 3 weeks to process your application. Please take note that the bank indicated in your GIRO-ON-Demand form must be a savings bank account.</li> 
                </ol> 
                <p></p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>I had submitted a GIRO-on-Demand application form but why is it not showing on the Payment Info? </strong><br> Processing time will take approximately 3 weeks. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Will I be notified when my GIRO-On-Demand (GOOD) is approved? </strong><br> Yes. After we have received your Application form for GIRO-On-Demand, we will send it to your bank for verification immediately. Upon the bank's approval, you will be notified through your registered email address that you can start paying your bills through SAM by GIRO-On-Demand. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Can I use more than one bank account for GIRO-On-Demand?</strong><br> No, only one Saving Bank Account can be used for bill payments. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Can I use somebody's bank account for GIRO-On-Demand?</strong><br> No, you will have to use your own bank account to pay bills through your SAM account. The name of the bank account holder on the Application for GIRO-On-Demand Service must be the same as the name you registered with SAM. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>What if I do not have enough money in my bank account?</strong><br> Once you authorize or schedule a payment, your bank will attempt to transfer the funds from your account on the third working day (subject to individual bank). If there are insufficient funds available to cover the transaction, your transfer may be denied. If this happens, we will notify you via email to inform you of the status. You then need to deposit sufficient funds into your account before another fresh payment is made. The regular bank charges may apply. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>What happens if I change my bank account?</strong><br> You will have to write to your bank to terminate the GIRO-On-Demand service for the existing bank account and submit a new Application for GIRO-On-Demand for your new bank account. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How do I cancel my GIRO-on-Demand account?</strong><br> You will have to write to your bank for the termination of GIRO-On-Demand. Upon receiving your bank's notification for termination, we will disable the pay button for GIRO-On-Demand and notify you the effective date. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>I have made a payment with GIRO-on-Demand awhile back but why is the status still showing "Submitted"?</strong><br> GIRO-on-Demand payments are settled offline, thus the status will not be updated. To find out the status of the payments, you will need to check with the billers. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Does SAM charge me for bill presentment &amp; payment services?</strong><br> No. SAM does not charge the consumers any service charge when you pay your bills. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How do I check the status for my payment?</strong><br> You can check the status of your payment by going into Payments -&gt; Payment History. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How do I dispute bill charges, if any?</strong><br> SAM is a payment aggregator and enables payments on behalf of our billers. All enquiries/disputes on bills should be taken up separately with the respective billers. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Why can't I see my amount due but I can see the overdue notice on my envelopes?</strong><br> Likewise in the physical world, the overdue amount is not shown on the envelopes. To protect your privacy, the overdue amount is shown only when you view your mail. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>How do I add/remove credit card details?</strong><br> Please login to SAM and click on the Profile menu link. From the Payment Info tab, you may add/remove your credit card details. </p> 
                <p class="linespace"> <strong>Why is the status of my credit card "Not Verified"?</strong><br> The credit card is reflecting as "Not Verified" because it has not has not been used to make payment. Once you have used the credit card for any payments, the credit card will reflect as "Verified". </p>

               <h3 class="contentheader">General</h3> 
               <p id="WHAT_IS_VBOX" class="linespace"> <strong>What is SAM?</strong><br> SAM started as a kiosk to bring our services closer so that you can pay bills, fines, top-ups, buy stamps, weigh your packages, and more even beyond the operating hours of our Post Offices. Today SAM extends beyond kiosk into multi-channel (Kiosks, Online and Mobile) to bring our services even closer and more accessible to you anytime and anywhere. </p>

Will there be any 24/7 support?</strong><br> Our contact details are:<br> Telephone: 1605<br> Telephone: +65 6841 2000 (when calling from outside Singapore)<br> Operations: <br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Monday -Friday: 8am to 6pm<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Saturday: 8am to 1pm<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sunday &amp; Public Holiday: Closed<br><br> The support provided will be on digital mailbox and payment related matters.
