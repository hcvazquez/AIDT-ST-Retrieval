       <a href="/en/blog/2017/07/download-content-go-announcing-support-offline-playback-brightcove-native-player-sdks" title="Download Content to Go! Announcing support for Offline Playback in the Brightcove Native Player SDKs" class="notranslate_title post-list-post-title"><h2 class="notranslate">Download Content to Go! Announcing support for Offline Playback in the Brightcove Native Player SDKs</h2></a>
       <div class="byline">
        <time class="post-date"><span class="SL_split">July 17, 2017</span></time>
        <span class="SL_split"> By </span>
        <a href="/en/blog/author/stewart-glass" class="post-author notranslate">Stewart Glass</a>
        <span class="notranslate byline-divider">&nbsp;&nbsp;|&nbsp;&nbsp;</span>
