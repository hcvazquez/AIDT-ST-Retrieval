              <li style="COLOR: #000000"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana">After the order ships, you will receive a&nbsp;2nd email with&nbsp;tracking information.</span></li>

             </ul> 
             <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0in; mso-add-space: auto"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; TEXT-DECORATION: underline; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; VERTICAL-ALIGN: auto; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: #000000; FONT-STYLE: normal; LINE-HEIGHT: 115%; BACKGROUND-COLOR: #ffffff; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial"><br> <br> Tracking your order:<br> <br></span></b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">Step 1:&nbsp; Sign in to your <strong style="TEXT-DECORATION: underline; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #0000ff">samsclub.com</strong> account.<br> <br></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">Step 2: &nbsp;Select "</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #000000; FONT-STYLE: italic"><b style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #0000ff">View</b> <b style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal"><span style="COLOR: #0000ff">Order History</span>"</b></span> <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">link under</span> <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #000000"><b style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-STYLE: italic">MY</b> <strong style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-STYLE: italic">Account</strong></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">.</span><br> <br> <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">Step 3:&nbsp; Click on "</span><b style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #000000; FONT-STYLE: italic">My Order History"</b> <span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">and&nbsp;narrow your results from the following</span><b style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana; COLOR: #000000">:<br></b></p>
