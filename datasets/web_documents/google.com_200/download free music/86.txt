    <h1 class="headline">Discover Why Beats Music is ‘Just Right’</h1> 
    <div id="banner"> 
     <img class="rounded" src="/content/dam/snr/2014/January 2014/beatsmusic_storelaunch_story.jpg.thumb.432.946.png" alt="FPO banner image"> 
    </div> 
    <div class="extra-info no-border clearfix"> 
     <p><a class="category lt-blue" href="/allpostpage.html?category=Wireless&amp;months=All&amp;page=1" target="_blank" title="">Wireless</a> / <span class="byline"> Jan 31, 2014</span></p> 
    </div> 
    <div id="main-content" class="left"> 
     <p>Get unlimited music downloads with Beats Music. Beats Music is an unlimited streaming music and offline listening subscription. Now AT&amp;T customers can get a month free subscription. </p> 
     <div class="story parsys">
      <div class="text parbase section">
       <p>There’s a new way for families to rock out on premium, personalized music at an affordable cost. Enter the exclusive family offer from <a href="http://beatsmusic.com/">Beats Music</a>. Sound like music to your ears? The best part is that this offer is now available in AT&amp;T stores nationwide.&nbsp;</p> 
       <p>Customers can now go in stores and <a href="http://att.com/beatsmusic">online</a> to get the music experience they’ve been waiting for:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><b>Family-friendly.</b></li> 
        <li><b>Unlimited streaming and downloads.</b></li> 
        <li><b>Human-curated content for the ultimate personalization.</b></li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>In the video below, <a href="http://www.ellentv.com/">Ellen Degeneres</a> shows us how <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i_eiTQy1QZw" target="_blank">Beats Music is ‘just the right fit.’</a></p> 
       <p><iframe width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/i_eiTQy1QZw"></iframe></p> 
       <p>With Beats Music – AT&amp;T customers on a multiline account get unlimited streaming music and downloads for offline listening for up to five family members across 10 devices. Each family member can get 24/7 access to more than 20 million songs and a unique curated experience, and the entire cost for the family is just $14.99 a month, exclusively from AT&amp;T.&nbsp;&nbsp; And it gets even better – you get 90 days free for your family – so you can try it and see how much your family can save.</p> 
       <p>See if Beats Music is the right fit for you and your family, by visiting your local AT&amp;T store or <a href="http://att.com/beatsmusic">att.com/beatsmusic</a>.</p> 
       <p><a adhocenable="false" href="http://about.att.com/newsroom/att_launches_exclusive_break_through_family_offer_from_beats_music.html">Read more</a> about this <a href="http://about.att.com/newsroom/att_launches_exclusive_break_through_family_offer_from_beats_music.html">exclusive offer</a> from Beats Music in our <a href="http://www.att.com/gen/press-room?pid=25200&amp;cdvn=news&amp;newsarticleid=37383">complete news release</a>.</p>
