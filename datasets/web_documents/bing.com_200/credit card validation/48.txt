         <div class="framed light">
          This is a simple class for validating credit card numbers.
          <br> 
          <br> It can perform MOD10 card number validation and also determine the credit card type based on the knowledge of the association the type to the combination of the card digits.
          <br> 
          <br> It supports validating cards by the following types: mastercard, discover, amex, diners, bankcard, jcb, enroute and switch.
