      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Public_health_and_biosurveillance">Public health and biosurveillance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_open-source_health_software&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Public health and biosurveillance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <ul> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Epi_Info" title="Epi Info">Epi Info</a> is <a href="/wiki/Public_domain" title="Public domain">public domain</a> statistical software for epidemiology developed by <a href="/wiki/Centers_for_Disease_Control_and_Prevention" title="Centers for Disease Control and Prevention">Centers for Disease Control and Prevention</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Spatiotemporal_Epidemiological_Modeler" title="Spatiotemporal Epidemiological Modeler">Spatiotemporal Epidemiological Modeler</a> is a tool, originally developed at <a href="/wiki/IBM_Research" title="IBM Research">IBM Research</a>, for modeling and visualizing the spread of infectious diseases. It is maintained by the <a href="/wiki/Eclipse_Foundation" title="Eclipse Foundation">Eclipse Foundation</a> and available under terms of the <a href="/wiki/Eclipse_Public_License" title="Eclipse Public License">Eclipse Public License</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>

       <li><a href="/wiki/Fast_Healthcare_Interoperability_Resources" title="Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources">Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources</a> (FHIR) is a <a href="/wiki/Health_Level_Seven_International" title="Health Level Seven International">Health Level 7</a> interoperability specification that defines <a href="/wiki/JSON" title="JSON">JSON</a> and <a href="/wiki/XML" title="XML">XML</a> data formats and a <a href="/wiki/Representational_state_transfer" title="Representational state transfer">RESTful</a> <a href="/wiki/Application_programming_interface" title="Application programming interface">API</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-93" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-93">[93]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-94" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-94">[94]</a></sup> It is available under the <a href="/wiki/Creative_Commons_license#Zero_.2F_public_domain" title="Creative Commons license">CC0 license</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-95" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-95">[95]</a></sup></li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/OpenEHR" title="OpenEHR">openEHR</a> is an open standard specification in health informatics that describes the management and storage, retrieval and exchange of health data in electronic health records (EHRs) following a two-level modeling paradigm. The OpenEHR base specification is available under the <a href="/wiki/Creative_Commons" title="Creative Commons">Creative Commons</a> Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported license.<sup id="cite_ref-96" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-96">[96]</a></sup></li> 
      </ul>
