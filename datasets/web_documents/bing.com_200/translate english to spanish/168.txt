           <p> Google Translate allows you to<strong> translate words, phrases, or texts</strong> from one language to another. </p> 
           <p> It offers numerous features, supports <strong>more than 60 languages</strong> ??(including English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, etc.), and can even translate while offline to a reasonable degree. </p> 
           <h3> Translated from texts, photos, drawings, or SMS </h3> 
           <p> Google Translate is a pocket translator that you can have confidence in. Write the words or phrases you want to translate, choose the original language, then the desired language, and it will <strong>convert the text in real-time</strong>. </p> 
           <p> The app also allows you to <strong>translate audio and words written on a whiteboard app</strong>. It also allows you to translate a text captured by the camera. </p> 
           <p> Google Translate <strong>allows for offline translations</strong>. To do this, you must download packages for the desired languages (about 200 MB each) from the configuration menu. After that, you will no longer need an Internet connection to translate - which is especially useful when traveling abroad. </p> 
           <h3> Minimalist interface design </h3> 
           <p> Google Translate can also <strong>translate SMS with ease</strong>. When you choose the option, you will be shown a list of all your received messages. Just select one, and the app will provide an immediate translation. </p> 
           <p> The program also shows the chronology of all your translations, allowing you to mark specific ones so you always have them to hand. Finally, Google Translate lets you <strong>listen to the pronunciation</strong> of both your entries and their translations. </p> 
           <p> Google Translate has a <strong>simple, minimalist design</strong>. Interface functions are well organized and easy to use, even for the inexperienced. </p> 
           <h3> The most complete translation app </h3> 
           <p> Translating with Google Translate is child's play. The <strong>quality of translations is good</strong>, although as with all similar apps translations may be inaccurate. </p> 
           <p> The ability to translate from texts, photos, SMS, or voice via dictation makes the program complete from all points of view. And the opportunity of being able to <strong>use it offline</strong> by downloading language packs make it essential for those who regularly need a translator for study, work, or pleasure. </p>
