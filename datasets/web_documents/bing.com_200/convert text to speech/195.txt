             <span style="display: block; font-size: 130%;"><strong>Text-to-Speech</strong></span>
             <br> 
             <strong>Why consider Text-to-speech (TTS)? Two reasons<span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark"> -</span></strong>
             <br> 
             <strong>1. It is essential for struggling readers; when text is digital it is now accessible and</strong>
             <br> 
             <strong>2. <span class="GRcorrect"><span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_correct"><span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark">it</span></span></span> supports the writing process, especially when editing and revising work</strong>
             <br> 
             <br> Note: Our databases are adding text-to-speech features for their articles. Check out 
             <a class="wiki_link_ext" href="http://ow.ly/745" rel="nofollow">Joyce's blog post</a>. Ask your librarian if the school offers any of the Gale products with text-to-speech features.
             <br> 
             <br> 
             <!-- ws:start:WikiTextHeadingRule:0:&lt;h3&gt; -->
             <h3 id="toc0"><a name="x--Mobile Devices"></a>
              <!-- ws:end:WikiTextHeadingRule:0 -->Mobile Devices</h3> 
             <ul>
              <li><span class="GRcorrect"><span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_spelling"><span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark">iOS</span></span></span> Devices have <strong><span class="GRcorrect"><span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark">Speak</span></span> Selection</strong> (TTS) with highlighting built in for iPads running <span class="GINGER_SOFATWARE_spelling"><span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark">iOS</span></span> 6 or 7. You must turn it on. <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark">Settings &gt; General</span> &gt; Accessibility &gt; Speak Selection &gt; On &gt; Highlight Words. Adjust the "Speaking Rate" to what works</li>
             </ul>
