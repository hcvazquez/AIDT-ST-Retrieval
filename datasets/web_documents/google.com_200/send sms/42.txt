     <h3>BULK SMS FAQ:</h3>
     <br> 
     <h6 class="mbr-section-title display-6">What are 2Factor's offerings?</h6> 
     <p><a href="http://2Factor.in/v3/" target="_blank">2Factor.in</a> offers simple &amp; reliable HTTP APIs for sending bulk SMS, transactional SMS, promotional SMS, OTP SMS for variery of use-cases ( Such As, Customer phone verification, Bulk SMS marketing / campaigns, Lead generation campaigns, SMS alerts etc. )</p> 
     <br> 
     <h6 class="mbr-section-title display-6">What is bulk sms sending / messaging / bulk sms marketing? How does bulk SMS help growing business?</h6> 
     <p>Bulk SMS sending also referred as <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulk_messaging" target="_blank">bulk sms messaging</a> simply means sending large number of SMS at once. A good bulk SMS Service provider provides with a simple interface to compose or schedule bulk sms messages in single click. Bulk SMS helps businesses and enterprises in communicating with their users, sharing important updates, up-selling / cross selling their products by executing effective marketing campaigns. Bulk messaging is widely used for transactions, promotions and mobile marketing.</p> 
     <br> 
     <h6 class="mbr-section-title display-6">What’s the difference between transactional and promotional SMS?</h6> 
     <p>Promotional / Marketing message typically refers to any message sent for promoting / up-selling / advertising your business offerings. Whereas Transactional / non-promotional sms refers to, messages sent to users as an acknowledgement to the actions performed by them on your app / website. <a href="http://blog.2factor.in/bulk-sms-otp-service/what-is-promotional-sms-vs-transactional-sms/" target="_blank">Read more</a> for better clarity. </p> 
     <br> 
     <h6 class="mbr-section-title display-6">What makes 2Factor the best bulk SMS provider?</h6> 
     <p>2Factor excels over competition in many ways making it the best Bulk SMS Gateway India. To put it simply, 2Factor offers below distinctive features. </p>
     <ol class=""> 
      <li><strong>Direct Connectivity:</strong> We manage direct connectivity with TIER-1 telcos, to provide best in class delivery time ( avg delivery time of 2-6 seconds )</li>
