       <p></p>
       <p></p>
       <p> </p> 
       <p><strong>CreditCard Validator</strong> is as the name suggests an application that is used in checking the validity of credit card numbers. Once you are in a line of business that requires you to accept credit cards, you will need a way to check their validity and this is an easy way to check while the customer is still available</p> 
       <p></p> 
       <p> </p> 
       <p></p>
       <p>The <strong>CreditCard Validator</strong> is able to check all the popular credit cards and more. You are able to check the validity of credit card numbers including MasterCard, Visa and Discover, among others. The good thing is that you do not have to be on the internet to check the card number as the application is still fully functional off-line.</p> 
       <p></p> 
       <p> </p> 
       <p></p>
       <p>The <strong>CreditCard Validator</strong> can automatically detect the type of card based on the first couple of numbers entered.</p> 
       <p></p> 
       <p>The CreditCard Validator will help you tell:<br></p> 
       <p>1. if a credit card number is valid,<br></p> 
       <p>2. which credit card vendor handles that number.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>It is a simple Applet to demonstrate the use of the ValidateCreditCard class.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>It validates the prefix and the checkdigit. It does *not* contact the credit card company to ensure that number has actually been issued and that the account is in good standing.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>It will also tell you which of the following credit card companies issued the card: Amex, Diners Club, Carte Blanche, Discover, enRoute, JCB, MasterCard or Visa.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>ATM cards use a different secret scheme which this program does not handle. Unfortunately, there is not even a reliable way to tell an ATM card from a credit card just by the number.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>It is primarily designed to be included in your own Java program, though it can be used standalone or as an Applet.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>To install, Extract the zip download with WinZip, available from winzip.com (or similar unzip utility) into any directory you please, often C:\ -- ticking off the (user folder names) option. To run as an application, type:<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>java -jar C:\com\mindprod\creditcard\creditcard.jar<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>adjusting as necessary to account for where the jar file is.<br></p> 
       <p><br></p> 
       <p>You must have Java version 1.1 or later installed to use this package.<br></p> 
       <p>See mindprod.com/jgloss/installingjava.html for details.</p>
       <p>System Requirements:</p>
       <p>Java 1.5+</p>
