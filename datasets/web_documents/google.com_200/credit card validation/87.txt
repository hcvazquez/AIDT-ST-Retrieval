       <h2>Capturing a Credit Card</h2> 
      </div> 
      <h2>Overview</h2> 
      <p>Capturing credit card details through the Assembly platform is a straightforward task, which can be accomplished by utilizing the PromisePay.js package. Through this integration, it is possible to capture card details and generate an accompanying token through a variety of different measures, including secure iFrame transport layers and custom forms.</p> 
      <p>&nbsp;</p> 
      <h2>Walkthrough</h2> 
      <h3>Before you start</h3> 
      <p>Capturing credit card information utilises the PromisePay.js package. Please make sure that you have properly set up the PromisePay.js integration on your platform before continuing.</p> 
      <p>If you have not yet set up PromisePay.js within your platform, please follow the instructions laid out in our guide for <a href="https://docs.assemblypayments.com/feature-guides/guides/integrating-promisepay-js/">Integrating PromisePay.js</a>.</p> 
      <h3>Capturing Credit Card Details</h3> 
      <p>Capturing credit card details through the front-end will mean data can be securely sent to Assembly with minimal PCI compliance impact. There are a couple of methods which can be used to capture credit card details.</p> 
      <h3>Generating a Card Token</h3> 
      <p>A card token is used to authorize the passing of credit card details through the client-side libraries. The generating of the card token occurs server-side, so that the generated token can be passed into the client-side for authentication. There are two use cases for generating a card token;</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Capturing credit card details of an existing user. This will involve passing the <code>user_id</code>parameter when generating the card token. This token will expire as soon as a valid credit card is added or after 2 hours.</li> 
       <li>Capturing credit card details for an anonymous or new user (using the Charge API). This will involve not passing the <code>user_id</code> parameter when generating the card token. This token will expire as soon as a valid credit card is added or after 2 hours.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <p>Use <a href="https://reference.assemblypayments.com/#generate-card-token">Generate Card Token</a> to generate the card token.</p>
