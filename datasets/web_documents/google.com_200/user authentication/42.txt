         <td>Does the application give useful debug information to the stacktrace or a log<br>file?</td>

         <td>Registration </td>
         <td>Users can sign in using just Facebook account (without registering first).</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>

         <td>Quality documentation in sphinx format </td>

         <td>The ability to find your facebook friends on this site</td>

        </tr>
